Our story

From garage to global

StrideX started in 2020 in a small garage in Winnipeg, Manitoba. Founders Fejiro Abere, Patrick Rukundo and Roopjeet Kaur were tired of choosing between ugly performance shoes and stylish kicks that fell apart. They set out to create a brand that refuses to compromise, shoes that look as good on the subway as they perform on the track.

Today, StrideX ships to over 120 countries, sponsors 15 professional athletes, and has been featured in Wired, GQ, and Runner's World. But our mission hasn't changed: push boundaries, break conventions, and make every step count.
